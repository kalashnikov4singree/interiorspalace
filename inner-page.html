Welcome to the Interiors Palace

Interiors Palace is a company that provides mill-work fabrication in residential, commercial, hospitality, cultural and retail projects. Our craftsmen can create custom furniture in fine finished hardwoods, high end laminates, fabrics and solid surface materials for kitchens, closets, vanities, wall units, libraries, banquettes, bars, offices, restaurants, stores, boutiques, private homes, and much more. ​
​
Other materials such as glass, acrylic, metal and lighting can be incorporated to satisfy the design and function. We carter to big and small projects no matter the obstacle. Our in house engineering and design department can help you transform your ideas into reality.

Some of our jobs include Greenhouse Nightclub, Juliet Supper Club, numerous Nine West Stores, as well as many private and commercial jobs. Everything is done in our very own family owned and operated manufacturing facility/showroom conveniently located in the heart of Brooklyn, minutes away from Manhattan, NY. ​
​
We encourage you to submit your project to us for quotation. Our quality and time delivery is guaranteed while offering competitive prices. From the design face to installation we are with you every step of the way.
